Get to Know Us

Empowering Communities Through Compassion & Action

Founded on September 23, 2020, by Founder & CEO Mr. Govind Shukla, the InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 licensed non-profit based in Chhattisgarh, India.

We hold registrations including 80G & 12A for tax benefits, CSR-1 registration for corporate collaborations, and enlistment with NITI Aayog. This rigorous standing underscores our absolute dedication to transparency, operational quality, and accountability.

Our Mission: To uplift marginalized communities and bridge socio-economic gaps through food security, accessible literacy, gender empowerment, stray animal care, and ecological conservation.

What We Do

Our Core Initiatives

InAmigos Foundation operates key structured initiatives to deliver systemic transformation across India.

Project Seva

Winter Relief & Food Security

Provides healthy meals, hygiene kits, and warm clothing to daily wage workers, homeless shelters, and low-income families.

  • Zero hunger in local slums
  • Blanket distributions in cold months
Current Progress 92% Achieved
"Serving Humanity" Support Seva
Project BachpanShala

Bridging Educational Gaps

Offers digital literacy, moral education, school supplies, and tuition support to children who are out of school or lag behind academically.

  • Setting up community study cells
  • Basic coding and computer classes
Current Progress 85% Achieved
"Nurturing Young Minds" Support BachpanShala
Project Jeev

Animal Protection & Care

Protects stray animals in urban zones. Our teams administer basic first aid, organize daily feeding runs, and build temporary shelters.

  • Road accident emergency response
  • Placing reflective collars on strays
Current Progress 78% Achieved
"Spreading Compassion" Support Jeev
Project Udaan

Women Empowerment

Provides vocational training in tailoring, sewing, and handicrafts while holding vital menstrual hygiene workshops and health camps in rural circles.

  • Vocational training & sewing machines
  • Sanitary pad manufacturing & awareness
Current Progress 80% Achieved
"Soaring High" Support Udaan
Project Prakriti

Eco-Conservation & Sustainability

Drives community reforestation, seedball making workshops, plastic collection operations, and educates farmers on organic composting.

  • Cleanliness campaigns in public parks
  • Maintenance of planted saplings
Current Progress 88% Achieved
"Plant for Tomorrow" Support Prakriti
Project Vikas

Youth Internships & Employability

Prepares young candidates for the workforce by providing practical, remote internships in marketing, research, finance, and technical development.

  • Mentoring over 10,000+ interns
  • Industry-recognized certifications
Current Progress 95% Achieved
"Shaping Skills" Support Vikas
Why We Exist

Addressing Systemic Crises with Sustained Solutions

Every community has unique hardships. Rather than applying short-term fixes, InAmigos Foundation deploys targeted campaigns designed to solve foundational issues and foster self-sufficiency.

Bridging Educational Inequality

Providing supplementary classes and resources to underserved students, preventing school dropout rates.

Fostering Animal Dignity

Preventing stray street animals from starving or dying of treatable road injuries through fast first-aid deployment.

Empowering Rural Women

Breaking cycles of dependency by establishing localized micro-enterprises and sewing workshops.

Why Volunteer With Us?

Volunteering at InAmigos Foundation is not just about charity; it is about self-development and professional learning. We offer a platform to expand your social vision while earning credible recognition.

Leadership Roles
